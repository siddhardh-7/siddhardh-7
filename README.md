# Why I Build

Technology evolves.

Frameworks come and go.

Great engineering principles don't.

I believe software becomes valuable when people stop noticing the technology behind it and simply enjoy the experience.

That's why I care about understanding problems deeply, building with intention, and refining products until the details disappear into the experience.

# What I Optimize For

Instead of chasing every new technology, I focus on things that continue to matter regardless of the stack.

- Building products people love using
- Performance users can actually feel
- Simplicity over unnecessary complexity
- Ownership from idea to production
- Learning faster than technology changes
